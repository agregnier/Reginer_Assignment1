Some brief discussion about Cheeseburgers and what they are

You'll come to notice that burgers can come in every different form, but here are the constants that Cheeseburgers have built on as a foundation:

  • Bun
  • Beef Patty
  • Cheese
  • Various sauces
  • Vegetable Garnish

The foundations of the burger have come to evolve over time and have created a culture that has evolved to be an international sensation. I first fell in love with them growing up in Southern California constantly visiting In'n'out. I feel that In'n'out has set the base of my burger passion. They never go out of the spectrum of the traditional burger, and from there I have grown to find what works best for burgers and what doesn't work. I look forward to getting deep in discussion of what makes the burger one of America's favorite dishes.
